# Getting Started

To start using Sessions Bot, [invite the bot](https://invite.sessionsbot.fyi) to any Discord server you <u>manage</u> or <u>own</u>.



### Who is it available for?

> Sessions Bot is available to any Discord user with permission to install applications into a server.


## First Steps

1. [Invite the Bot](https://invite.sessionsbot.fyi) to a server you _manage or own_.
2. Grant Sessions Bot all [required permissions](/bot-permissions) during the invite process. 
3. Visit your [Bot Dashboard](https://sessionsbot.fyi/dashboard) and sign in with your Discord account.
4. Create your first [`Session`](/sessions), customized however you like.
5. Wait for your [`Session Panel`](/sessions#session-panels) to be posted at its scheduled time.



## Using your Dashboard

To configure Sessions Bot and manage your server, use the online [Bot Dashboard](https://sessionsbot.fyi/dashboard).

Some things to know:
- You must sign in with your Discord account to access your dashboard data.
- **Once signed in**, you can create sessions, view past session history, update server preferences, and more.



> [!CAUTION] ⓘ PLEASE NOTE:
> Data **will not** remain available in your dashboard *(cloud)* indefinitely. For long-term records, refer to your posted [`Session Panels`](/sessions#session-panels) within your Discord server.
