# Subscriptions

Sessions Bot is **free to use for anybody**, however some limited <u>exclusive features</u> are available for purchase.

### Available Plans

- Free
- Premium
- Enterprise

## Competitive Limits
Compared to our largest competitors we offer higher limits for things like active schedules and customization.
> To view specific limit quotas by plan view our [Pricing](https://sessionsbot.fyi/pricing) page.

## Purchasing & Refunds
To purchase/upgrade your bot's subscription plan visit our [Discord Marketplace Store](https://discord.com/discovery/applications/1137768181604302848/store). Here is where you can **purchase, manage, and cancel** any subscription you currently have.
